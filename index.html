The 5th Asian Conference on Pattern Recognition (ACPR 2019)

26-29 November 2019, Auckland, New Zealand

We welcome you to Auckland, New Zealand for the 5th Asian Conference on Pattern Recognition (ACPR 2019), to be held on 26-29 November 2019.
ACPR is a premier level international conference that brings together researchers in pattern recognition and related fields. Submissions are not restricted to those from Asia-Pacific, and are highly encouraged.

Topics of interest include all aspects of pattern recognition, including, but not limited to:
- Computer Vision and Robot Vision
- Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning
- Signal Processing (signal, speech, image)
- Media Processing and Interaction (videos, documents, medical, biometrics, HCI, VR, etc.)

The conference will be held at the Aotea Centre in Auckland. ACPR 2019 is brought to you by the Auckland University of Technology (AUT), in collaboration with the University of Otago and the Indian Statistical Institute, with support from ATEED, Auckland Council, and New Zealand Tourism.

The proceedings will be published in Springer's Lecture Notes in Computer Science Springer's Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS, indexed in the ISI Conference Proceedings Citation Index, Scopus, EI Engineering Index, Google Scholar, DBLP, etc.). ACPR 2019 is endorsed by the International Association for Pattern Recognition (IAPR).
 

For any queries at this early stage, please contact acpr2019@aut.ac.nz.
