--- title: ACPR 2019 - The 5th Asian Conference on Pattern Recognition ---

Conference Venue

ACPR 2019 starts on the evening of November 25 with a welcome reception, including a traditional welcoming ceremony, at AUT’s WG Building. Five workshops on 26 November are also taking place in this building (see program page for details). ACPR 2019 tutorials, three workshops, and the main conference will be held at the amazing Aotea Centre, the largest modern tiered auditorium in New Zealand. It is centrally located in the Auckland CBD, within walking distance to many hotels and restaurants (AUT’s WG Building is only a five-minutes walk away from Aotea Centre).
