--- title: ACPR 2019 - The 5th Asian Conference on Pattern Recognition ---

Conference Program

The programme will be single track, including keynotes, oral presentations, posters, tutorials, workshops and demos as in previous conferences. We expect more than 400 submissions with an acceptance rate below 50 per cent. Three internationally renowned researchers will be invited as keynotes. Their travel, accommodation and registration will be paid for by the conference. Approximately 100 researchers will be asked to join the programme committee.

The proceedings will be published by IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS). The publisher will be confirmed by the programme chairs in due course. The proceedings will be available at the Conference in electronic form. The best papers will be invited to submit to a journal special issue - details of the journal and the process will be determined prior to 2019.
